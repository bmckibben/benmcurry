The Science of Miracles

A toddler and family are abducted by extraterrestrials for the purpose of raising the child in an alien culture.

A fleet of UFOs appears to menace the population of earth. The social, political, financial anomie that follows prompts a revolution against a government that has become a kleptocracy.

The revolution becomes a totalitarian regime. Those who do not join the one political party are marginalized.

The grown-up toddler returns to earth to share alien knowledge. The government takes credit for the sharing with the alien race and promotes the alien as an affirmation of the government's legitimacy.

The alien becomes disillusioned with the government and seeks refuge with an underground resistance movement, taking advantage to disseminate the alien knowledge through the resistance’s network.

The alien befriends a former physicist who has created a device to access higher dimensions. Using the alien knowledge they are able to enhance the device to allow a person to transport themselves from one location to another using the higher dimensions as a shortcut. Together they help the resistance until the government learns of the device.

The Government steals the device then kidnaps the alien and tortures the alien to explain how the device works.

The physicist discovers the device is attuned to him only and that he doesn’t need it to teleport. The physicist rescues the alien.

The alien is so badly injured it must return to its planet it came from.
